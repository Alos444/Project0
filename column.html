BAGDAT AVENUE or, shortly, the Avenue is located in the Asian Side of Istanbul. Being in Kadıköy district, the Avenue runs along from Bostancı to Bostancı Bridge in Kızıltoprak. This avenue has great importance not only for the residents of Istanbul but also local and foreign tourists. The 9-kilometer avenue is among those places that are the heart of Istanbul. What can you do and eat on Bağdat Avenue, one of the most popular avenues in Istanbul?
MAIDEN TOWER It literally means "Maiden's Tower" in Turkish. The name comes from a legend: the Byzantine emperor heard a prophecy telling him that his beloved daughter would die at the age of 18 by a snake. So he decided to put her in this tower built on a rock on the Bosphorus isolated from the land thus no snake could kill her. But she couldn't escape from her destiny after all, a snake hidden in a fruit basket brought from the city bit the princess and killed her.
CADDEBOSTAN BEACHES connected to Kadıköy Municipality, which consists of 3 public beaches, one of which is paid, is the easiest accessible beach for those who live near Kadıköy on the Anatolian side of Istanbul. All three of Caddebostan Beaches have sun loungers, umbrellas, locker cabins, showers and lifesaving towers, WC and a buffet. The sandy beaches are also very suitable for families with children. It is a great advantage to have large green areas behind the beach area where you can have a picnic in the shade of the tree.
